How Car Shipping Works

Booking

Choose a pickup and delivery window, provide vehicle information, and select the shipping option (open or enclosed). We'll confirm availability and send a transparent quote.

Pickup

Carrier dispatch coordinates a pickup. Driver inspects your vehicle and records condition before loading. We confirm pickup time and driver details before arrival.

Shipping

Your vehicle is transported across state lines on scheduled routes. Our carriers communicate regularly and provide updates during transit, ensuring safe handling.

Delivery

Carrier notifies the recipient ahead of arrival. A final inspection is performed at delivery and proof of delivery is provided. Any questions? We stay available until you're satisfied.

Services

Open Auto Shipping

Open carrier car shipping is the standard method of transporting a vehicle. Your vehicle will be transported on an open car carrier, commonly seen delivering new cars to dealerships. If your move consists of transporting standard daily vehicles the open transport is your preferred choice and is the most cost effective way of transporting your vehicle. Most daily vehicles are exposed to weather elements and do not require the added expense of an enclosed transport. Regardless of which option you choose we strive on providing excellence when transporting your vehicle. However, because the vehicles on the open carrier are not enclosed during transport, customers should beware that their car is not safeguarded from possible road perils. The vehicles on the carrier are exposed to rough weather, fly away debris, rocks, dirt, fluid drips, or other hazards.

Enclosed Auto Shipping

An enclosed auto carrier provides maximum security for your vehicle during transportation. If you're shipping exotic, luxury, classic or custom vehicles our enclosed transport service would be your preferred option. Enclosed auto transport will protect your car and insure complete safety from any type of weather or road conditions that may occur during shipping. Our enclosed carriers range in size so no matter what type of car you own we can ship it. Whenever shipping with Auto Shipping Experts you will have the security of knowing your precious cargo is in the right hands.

Boats Shipping

Door-to-door boat transportation service in continental US. Many boats are shipped via drive-away (must be on a trailer). Other options like flat-bed, step-deck and low-boy are available to accommodate most boat types. Contact us for custom quotes and equipment requirements.

Motorcycles Shipping

Door-to-door motorcycle shipping in continental US. Your motorcycle can be shipped either crated or uncrated, and will travel with full insurance to its new destination. Open and enclosed carriers are available to accommodate the relocation of any motorcycle, sport bike, or chopper.

RV Shipping

We offer door-to-door RV transport solutions across the continental US. From travel trailers to motorhomes, our carriers and equipment are selected to safely handle larger vehicles. Each RV move is assessed for size, clearance, and required permits to ensure a smooth, on-time delivery.

Inoperable Vehicles Shipping

Non-running vehicle? No problem. We specialize in transport of inoperable vehicles for auctions, tow yards, and insurance shops. If the car does not start a winch is generally used. For heavily damaged vehicles a roll-back or forklift may be required at pickup or delivery. Customers should inform the hauler ahead of time so proper equipment is ready. Whenever shipping cars without keys, wheels should be straight and transmission set to neutral if possible.
